Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2024

Welcome to Grandma Sudoku. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Grandma Sudoku is designed to be played offline and requires no account creation. We collect minimal data to provide and improve the app experience:

  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics such as puzzle completion rates and session duration, collected to improve difficulty balance and performance.
  • Device information: Operating system version and device type, used only for compatibility purposes.
  • Crash reports: Automatic crash logs to help us fix bugs quickly.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you explicitly contact us for support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Maintain and improve the app's stability and performance.
  • Understand how players interact with puzzles and features.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues.

We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Grandma Sudoku may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting (e.g., Firebase). These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services.

4. Advertising

The free version of Grandma Sudoku may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies or device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising through your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Grandma Sudoku is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 24 months and then automatically deleted. Crash reports are retained for up to 12 months.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@grandmasudoku.app
